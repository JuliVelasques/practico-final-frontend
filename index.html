Introduction

Rick and Morty is an American animated series brought to the screen by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. The television network that made her appearance possible was Adult Swim. Its creators were based on Back to the Future. The plot basically deals with the adventures of Rick, a super-intelligent and pessimistic scientist, and his grandson Morty, a shy and impressionable 14-year-old teenager.

Synopsis:

The series follows the misadventures of cynical mad scientist Rick Sanchez and his kind-hearted but restless grandson Morty Smith, who divide their time between domestic life and adventures interdimensional events that take place in an infinite number of realities, often traveling to other planets and dimensions through portals and in Rick's flying saucer. The concept The general story of Rick and Morty is based on two conflicting settings: the domestic family drama and a alcoholic grandfather dragging his grandson into mischief.

Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon.

Launch: 2013.

Country of origin: USA.
